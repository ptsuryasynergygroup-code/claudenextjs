====================================================
PRODUCT VISION
====================================================

EOS adalah platform operasi perusahaan terpadu yang menyatukan:

- Organization
- People
- Workflow
- Documents
- Tasks
- Finance
- Procurement
- CRM
- Inventory
- Assets
- Risk
- Analytics
- AI

Tujuan utama:
1. Single Source of Truth
2. Workflow Driven Operation
3. Audit First Architecture
4. AI Ready Architecture
5. Modular Enterprise Platform
